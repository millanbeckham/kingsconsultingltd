About Kings Consulting

Kings Consulting Supplies & Investment Ltd is a regional consultancy firm incorporated in Kenya to transform organizations through high-quality consultancy, training, research, monitoring & evaluation, supplies, and investment support.

We work with public institutions, private companies, NGOs, and regional organizations across Kenya and other African countries including Tanzania, Uganda, Ethiopia, Rwanda, Sudan, and South Sudan.

Our Vision

To be a leading consulting firm that empowers organizations with innovative, practical, and impactful solutions for sustainable growth.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality consultancy, training, research, supplies, and investment support that enhances organizational performance, governance, and impact.
